Daramad Booking App product design

Daramad Booking App is a mobile application designed for employees and managers who want to overview their reserved hours, what they will do, and when they aren't working.

Problem Statement:

Most employees and managers write their day, week, or monthly plans on simple notebooks or planners. On paper, they cannot search, edit or delete the schedules. There isn't any intelligence to warn them if they already have a plan at a specific time.The other important issue they encounter is that if everyone has their book, how will they manage the sources that anyone might need them?

Ideation:

  • it needs to be simple because users already have a straightforward solution (write their everyday plan on a whiteboard in their office).
  • it needs to be aesthetic because most of the users care.
  • we have to choose between one day, one week, or even one month view on the calendar.
  • we could set a break or time off easily.
  • we have to choose the sources and time of the service we give to the customers.

Design Challenge:

How do we choose a calendar view for one person for the whole month and all team members?

Solution:

If the user chooses one person, we can show her the whole month, but if the manager decides to see all members' schedules, she can view just one day of their plans and reservations.

Hi-Fi Design

I design the main flow of the app and then prototype it. I conducted a small research on random people and a few people who match our product's persona and how they work with our app, and I found one pain point.

The pain point was users didn't find how to add new staff to their calendars. ( in the first version, they add a new team member via a setting tab in the sidebar)
